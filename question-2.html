Background Information

Dong Mai resides in Chi Phat village located deep within the Cardamom Mountains at Koh Kong Province, south-west Cambodia.

The villagers experience poor sanitation due to lack of proper toilet facilities in the village. There are currently two communal toilets built by volunteers 6 years ago.

The villagers are facing a surge in cases of Cholera as both communal toilet has broken down.
